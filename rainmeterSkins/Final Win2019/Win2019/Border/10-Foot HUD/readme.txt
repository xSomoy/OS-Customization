"10-Foot HUD" Skin for Rainmeter
Created by Stephen R Gibson

This pack includes two parts; the Date & Time and Weather*, both in black and white versions.

To install: Just drop the "10-Foot HUD" folder into your Rainmeter "Skins" folder. You'll need to edit the URL for weather data, instructions are in the script.


*Weather skin based on Moxaweather.
